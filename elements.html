What Inspired Me to Pursue Software Engineering?

Personal Reasoning

I chose to be a Software Engineer because I enjoy having the ability to use my creative ideas to build something that could advance the world of technology. I also like the fact that something new can be learned or generated as a result of any progress made. I want to help build the future through technology and software engineering was the most optimal choice to achieve this desire.

Software Development is a creative and adaptable field

Since software engineering is a relatively new field, you never know what new apps or programs will need to be developed. The unpredictability of what will be essential technology in the future is what intrigues me to want to be apart of such an expandable field. Problem-Solving and spontaneity are two qualities that describe my personality well so the adaptability needed for a career in software engineering directly aligns with my personal interests.
