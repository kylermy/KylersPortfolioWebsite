About Me Page

My name is Kyler Martin-Yeboah and I am a 20 year old Computer Science major with a Digital Media & Arts minor at Louisiana State University. I am orginally from Baltimore, Maryland where I lived until leaving for college. I was exposed to the computer science field at a young age after attending various cybersecurity and coding camps.

I chose to major in Computer Science because I liked the creative and collaborative atmosphere aimed at increasing future technological potential. Computer Science is a very diverse field that contains many different aspects all needing separate coding languages and algorithms to perform the prospective function. The freedom to choose which areas intrigue me, learn new ways to advance technology while still being able to incorporate my own design or structure ideas, and stay up to date on the current available technology which help streamline or generate new projects; are some other reasons I admire the career path. I'd like to think my affinity for sci-fi also plays apart in my pursuit of the computer science field as many of the fictional aspects relate to computer science in some way. Overall, I have a large vested interest in learning as much as I can about computer science and contributing my ideas to grow and advance the field for the future.
